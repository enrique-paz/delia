Simple Installation

LumaNEXT™ is the first and only low voltage system that requires no transformers, bulbs or ballast at the fixture. Current is distributed from centralized power stations through DC distribution hubs. Placed strategically throughout the store, these hubs simplify wiring, seamlessly feed current and optimize power usage. The system’s plug-and-play technology eliminates the need for special tools or special training and is virtually maintenance free.

Unmatched Light Quality

LumaNEXT™ solid-state overhead luminaires can be easily installed in drop ceilings, suspended openly or truss mounted. They feature a proprietary Evonik Diffuser lens and special coatings by White Optics for greater diffusion, outstanding reflectivity and outstanding light quality.

Total System

LumaNEXT™ is the only low voltage illumination solution designed to enhance the entire retail experience. The solid-state system includes ceiling lighting, suspended signs, track lighting, illuminated perimeter wall displays and merchandisers. The low voltage power grid allows “plug & play” installation of all devices.

Solar & Sustainable

The LumaNEXT system is solar power compatible, seamlessly delivering uninterrupted power through the LumaNEXT Power Source Manager. Available integrated sensors monitor the system for daylight harvesting & energy conservation. Thanks to its modular design and high efficiency LumaNEXT™ has a much lower carbon footprint and requires significantly less energy to operate.

Control & Analysis

Enlighted Smart Sensors measure ambient light and temperature, analyze consumer foot traffic and report fixture outages with the ability to generate actionable data reports. Store staff can control lighting intensity within departments based on desired mood, display strategy and shopping patterns. The result is more effective illumination at significantly reduced energy costs.

Future-Proof & Compliant

In addition to its extremely low power consumption, LumaNEXT™ features patent-pending open architecture to accommodate any future system expansion and technology updates. LumaNEXT™ lighting control devices, luminaries and systems fully comply with all Title 24 requirements*.

*Title 24 – California 2013 Building Energy Efficiency Standards for Residential and Non-Residential Buildings, pp. 104-106
